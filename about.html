About Our Project

There are many international organizations that collect population health statistics. Our project uses these data to help individuals assess their own health. By comparing your personal indicators with global statistics from the World Health Organization’s Global Health Observatory (WHO-GHO) [link], Europe-specific data from WHO-Europe [link], and international health and demographic indicators from the World Bank Open Data API [link], you can monitor your health, consult specialists when needed, and make informed decisions to improve well-being. In addition, we support you by providing the possibility to find qualified specialists in your country who can help you manage changes in your health parameters. The goal of this project is to provide a simple, evidence-based tool for proactive health management.
